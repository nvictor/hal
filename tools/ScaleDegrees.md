# Scale Degrees

**Tonic**: The main note, the key's center and resting point.
**Supertonic**: The note "above the tonic".
**Mediant**: Sits in the middle (mediant) of the tonic and dominant.
**Subdominant**: Under the dominant (sub-), often preparing for the dominant.
**Dominant**: Creates tension and strongly pulls to the tonic.
**Submediant**: Middle note between the upper tonic and subdominant.
**Leading Tone**: A half-step below the tonic, creating strong pull (or Subtonic in natural minor).

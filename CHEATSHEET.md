# HFN Cheatsheet (v1.0.0)

## Center State (Prefix)
- `=X`: Static center
- `⇄X`: Oscillating center
- `⊃X`: Expanded center
- `↗X`: Intensifying toward X
- `→X`: Moving to new center X

## Chord Motion (In-line)
- `=`: Sustain / Pedal
- `⇄`: Oscillation
- `→`: Directed progression

## Modifiers
- `↑`: Shift up octave
- `↓`: Shift down octave
- `,`: Phrase separator
